TECHNICAL DEFINITION: MLUPS

Mega Lattice Updates Per Second (MLUPS) is the measurement for simulation throughput. It represents the volume of numerical updates processed by the GPU core every second. A performance of 50+ MLUPS indicates over 50 million discrete cell states computed per second.
